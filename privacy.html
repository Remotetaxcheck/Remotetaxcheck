Privacy Policy

Last updated: June 2026

The short version

RemoteTaxCheck does not collect your personal data. The risk assessment tool runs entirely in your browser. Nothing you enter is sent to our servers or stored anywhere.

Information we do not collect

When you use the risk assessment tool, all processing happens locally in your browser using JavaScript. We do not collect, transmit, or store:

  • The countries you enter
  • Your employment or contractor status
  • Any other information you input into the tool

Information we may collect automatically

Like most websites, we use standard analytics tools (Google Analytics) that may collect anonymised data including:

  • Pages visited and time spent on each page
  • General geographic location (country level only)
  • Device type and browser
  • How you found the site (search engine, direct link, etc.)

This data is anonymous and aggregated. We use it only to understand which content is most useful and to improve the site.

Cookies

We use a single functional cookie to remember your dark or light mode preference. This contains no personal information. We also use Google Analytics cookies for anonymised traffic analysis, and Google AdSense cookies for advertising. You can control cookie preferences through your browser settings.

Google AdSense

We display advertisements served by Google AdSense. Google may use cookies and similar technologies to serve ads based on your prior visits to this and other websites. You can opt out of personalised advertising by visiting Google's Ads Settings.

Third party links

This site may link to external websites. We are not responsible for the privacy practices of those sites. We recommend reviewing the privacy policy of any external site you visit.

Your rights

Since we do not collect personal data, there is nothing to access, correct, or delete. For questions about the anonymised analytics data, please contact us via the contact page.

Changes to this policy

We may update this policy occasionally. The date at the top of this page will reflect when it was last changed.

Contact

For privacy-related questions, please use our contact page.
